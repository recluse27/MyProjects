Create .bat file that will do next actions:
1. Unzip archive and rename pictures;
2. Concatenation channel visible, near and middle infrared spectral range images into one HEOTIFF. Download library from gdal.org. Download pictures from http://earthexplorer.usgs.gov;
3. Project Satellite image at the specified coordinate system mapping;
4. Concatenation images of two adjacent 'row' with the same 'path';
5. Trime the resulting images for a given size.
